Travel Smart

Kumbh Yatra Essential Checklist

Make sure to check these essentials before starting your holy journey.

🪪

Valid ID Proof

Carry original and photocopies of your Aadhar Card or Voter ID.

💊

Basic Medical Kit

Include pain relief, fever meds, and your regular prescriptions.

🧣

Warm Clothing

Nashik nights can be cold; carry a light sweater or shawl.

🔋

Portable Power Bank

Phone batteries drain fast in crowds; keep a charged power bank.

🧴

Personal Hygiene Kit

Carry sanitizer, small soap, and a towel for use after the holy dip.

💰

Emergency Cash

Network issues might cause UPI failures; keep some physical cash.

Safety First

Important Guidelines

Follow these rules for your own safety and the safety of others at the Kumbh Mela.

✅ What to do (Do's)

  • After bathing at the ghat, immediately vacate the place so that other people can take bath.
  • Always dispose of waste in dustbins and keep the Godavari river clean.
  • Always carry an ID card and emergency contact number with you.
  • Follow the instructions of police and volunteers.
  • Hold the hands of your children and elderly people in crowded places.

❌ What not to do (Don'ts)

  • Do not use soap or shampoo in the river at all.
  • Do not enter deep water (gehre paani), always stay within barricades.
  • Do not take food or drinks from unknown people.
  • Do not throw plastic, clothes or any religious items in the river.
  • Do not believe rumors and do not create panic in crowded places.
