Kumbh Travel Guidelines

Essential roadmap for a safe and divine experience

1. Before You Travel

  • Register on official portal if required
  • Double-check Shahi Snan dates
  • Book stay at least 3 months early
  • Carry original ID + 2 Photocopies
  • Keep Cash (Network issues in crowds)

2. What to Carry

Power bank Water bottle Comfy Shoes Basic Meds Cap/Umbrella

3. During Visit

  • Follow crowd control & barricades
  • Keep family members' phone numbers in pockets
  • Reach Ghats 3 hours before Aarti/Snan

4. Bathing Guidelines

Warning: Use only approved ghats.

  • Don't go into deep water (Follow chains)
  • Listen to Police/Volunteers on Megaphones
  • Avoid using soap/shampoo in holy water

5. Stay & Food

  • Book only verified Hotels or Ashrams
  • Drink only sealed/bottled water
  • Avoid overcrowded street stalls to prevent infection

6. Transport & Safety

  • Use Shuttle buses (Private vehicles restricted)
  • Beware of scams: Don't trust strangers with bags
  • Expect 5-8km of walking during peak dates

7. Family & Seniors

  • Avoid Peak Hours (10 AM - 4 PM) for elderly
  • Set a fixed 'Meeting Point' if anyone gets lost
  • Wheelchair help available at official booths

8. Emergency Help

Police: 100
Ambulance: 108
Lost & Found: 1912
